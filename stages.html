Plan:

Before we start, the first step is to get you situated with all of the paperwork as well as move you to one of our locations. After that, we will first start off by asking you about your desired position. These can include positions such as pilot astronauts, mission specialists astronauts, and other jobs. Then, we will assess you on your knowledge of this position. We are doing this because if you have a specific position in mind, you should also have a foundation so that we can build on top of that to create an ideal astronaut.

Training:

Your training regime will consist of classes, tests, research, hands-on training, and other activites that span over 8 hours everyday from Monday to Friday. You will be re-visiting basic information so that we can solidify your foundation. Then we ill supplement it with more information. Saturday will be used as a catch-up day if you needed any extra help or need to re-attempt something. Sunday will be used as a break. Based on your porformance on the tests and activities, you may have a chance to move onto the next stage earlier.

Safety Prep:

During this stage, you will be working with your teachers as well as your peers to increase and practice your knowledge on safety. Space may be very interesting, but it is also very dangerous. Passing or failing this stage may also reflect your choices later on. So, we take extra care in this stage so that you master safety procedures and information.

Launching:

This next stage will be focused on your actual training in a space like environment. Based on your membership plan, this stage can allow you to do things anywhere from VR experience to cutting edge gravitational technology that will make you feel like you are in space. This stage is where you have to prove yourself and show us what you know. Based on your performance, you may even be sent into space for a short amount of time using our state of the art reusable space shuttle.

Recovery:

In this final stage, you will review everything you have learned during your time at our agency. This final stage will be more relaxed. There will be one more final test to see how much you have progressed since you first took your test. Then you will be given a choice on which space agency you want to join, and we will help you get there. After completing this stage, you have finally graduated our agency, becoming the brilliant and bright astronauts you always wanted to become!

Costs

In order to train you to be an excellent astronaut and get you employed by NASA, we need to spend a lot of money, and in order to fund that, we have created four different membership plans to allow various options for everybody while at the same time allowing us to maintain our quality.

Plans

Membership Plans

We have 4 different memebership plans for you to choose from: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platnium. The higher the rank of the plan, the more benefits that are provided to you. You can click here to get directed to the Membership Plans page.

Training Costs

After you select a plan, you have to select a tutor. We have some of the best tutors in the industry with years of experience under their belt. You can click here to view the instructors you can choose from.

Facility Costs

We have three main facilities located in Japan, China, and the U.S.A. The facility you get to use depends on the plan you select and the larger the facility, the more advantages you get. You can view more info about the facilities here.

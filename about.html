Who are we:

We are an Astronaut Training Agency

Space exploration is the future, and we believe that brilliant astronauts will be the ones to lead us into that future. Our agency will be training future astronauts to make them the best in their field so that one day, they may help us uncover the vast unknowns of space.

Our mission/goal

Our mission is to help as many people become astronauts as possible. We want to help the people around the world achieve their goals of going to space and getting a feel of how it is like there. We want to help them satiate their curiosity of how different things work in space and on earth.

We are commited to helping you and other people around the world become astronauts who can lead the future of humanity. Astronauts who can bring humanity one step closer to learning more and more about the universe.

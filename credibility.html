Our success stories:

Meet Jose. Jose is a veteran astronaut with over 35 years of experience. He has made a trip to the moon and has helped with the success of The Magellan which was the first spacecraft to image the entire surface of Venus and made several discoveries about the planet. And his journey started with us all the way back in 1987 when we were just starting. He is now one of the teachers that you can learn from if you select the platnium plan.

Meet John. John is another veteran astronaut with over 10 years of experience and he is also a teacher here for gold and platnium members. He has worked with NASA for the last 5 years and has decided to help nurture astronauts with the agency that helped nurture him.

And finally meet Bellam. Bellam had always wanted to be a astronaut ever since he was young and so he applied to learn under us. After 3 years of practice and experience, he got a job at NASA earning 120k/year. He says "I couldn't have done it without space tourist".
